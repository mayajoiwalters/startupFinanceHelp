About The Company

The purpose of this company is to help people who are wanting to create a start-up business make sure they are financially stable. The process will involve them entering a series of information and the system will calculate it to determine if the business is going to be financially stable, needs more work, more money is need, etc.

About The Creator

Maya Joi Walters is a sophomore at Fisk University. She is a Business Administration major with a concentration in Financial Economics. With her passion being towards financial consulting, she saw fit that this website would begin her journey to her financial consulting career in the future.
